Annexure A

1. Overview of Company and Affiliates

FanVerse Inc. is a British Virgin Islands international business company whose business is to operate an NFT marketplace for content creators and influencers.

2. Overview of Network and Tokens

The FanVerse Network is an NFT marketplace and a custom NFT launchpad with a mission to provide influencers and their fans a place where influencers have control over their content while fans get greater and more fulfilling experiences through the various consumer touchpoints that bring the user and creator closer together. FanVerse Tokens enable use of the FanVerse Network by serving as the token required for in-platform micropayments, NFT bidding/purchasing, staking and the payment of marketplace fees while incentivising the development of communities and the sustenance of engagement through user-creator centric social activities. This is implemented through dynamic staking pools that allow both users and influencers to passively earn FanVerse Tokens via the different types of NFTs that can be staked on the FanVerse Network.

Any creator or influencer can create digital assets (also known as NFTs) on the FanVerse Network. Further details pertaining to the FanVerse Network may be found on the published whitepaper available on the Website.
